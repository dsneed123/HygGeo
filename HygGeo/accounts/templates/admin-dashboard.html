SEO-Optimized Experience Titles

Generate compelling, SEO-friendly titles for travel experiences

AI Prompt:
Create 5 SEO-optimized titles for a [EXPERIENCE_TYPE] experience in [DESTINATION]. Each title should: - Include the destination name - Be 30-60 characters long - Include power words like "authentic", "unique", "sustainable", "hidden gem" - Appeal to eco-conscious travelers - Include hygge elements when relevant - Focus on emotional benefits Example format: "Authentic [EXPERIENCE] in [DESTINATION]: Sustainable [ACTIVITY] Experience"
Meta Descriptions

Create compelling meta descriptions that improve click-through rates

AI Prompt:
Write a meta description for a travel experience titled "[TITLE]" in [DESTINATION]. Requirements: - Exactly 140-155 characters - Include the focus keyword "[KEYWORD]" - Include a clear call-to-action (book, discover, explore) - Mention sustainability/eco-friendly aspects - Create urgency or exclusivity - Appeal to hygge philosophy (mindful, authentic, peaceful travel) Example: "Discover authentic [keyword] in [destination]. Book sustainable, locally-guided experiences that support communities. Mindful travel starts here."
Experience Descriptions

Generate detailed, SEO-optimized experience descriptions

AI Prompt:
Create a comprehensive description for a [EXPERIENCE_TYPE] in [DESTINATION]. Include: **Structure (400-800 words):** 1. Hook opening with emotional appeal 2. Detailed activity description 3. Sustainability/local impact benefits 4. Hygge elements (comfort, mindfulness, simplicity) 5. Practical details (duration, group size, difficulty) 6. What's included/excluded 7. Call-to-action ending **SEO Requirements:** - Use keyword "[KEYWORD]" 3-5 times naturally - Include related keywords: sustainable travel, eco-friendly, authentic, local community - Use headers and bullet points - Write for 8th-grade reading level - Include questions to boost engagement **Tone:** Inspiring, authentic, informative, appealing to conscious travelers
Keyword Research

Research and suggest relevant keywords for travel experiences

AI Prompt:
Generate a comprehensive keyword list for a [EXPERIENCE_TYPE] experience in [DESTINATION]. Provide: **Primary Keywords (3-5):** - Main focus keywords with destination + activity - Consider search volume and competition **Long-tail Keywords (8-10):** - Specific phrases travelers would search - Include sustainability, hygge, and authenticity angles **Local SEO Keywords (5-7):** - Location-specific terms - "Near me" variations - Regional/cultural terms **Competitor Analysis:** - Research what similar experiences rank for - Identify keyword gaps and opportunities **Semantic Keywords:** - Related terms Google associates with your main keywords - Include synonyms and related concepts Format as a prioritized list with estimated search difficulty (low/medium/high).
