HygGeo operates on an affiliate commission model, similar to platforms like TripAdvisor or Booking.com.

Here's how it works:

  • When you book through links on our site, we may earn a small commission from the booking platform or tour operator
  • This commission comes from their marketing budget - it doesn't increase your price
  • You pay the same price as booking directly (sometimes even less with exclusive deals)
  • This model allows us to offer our platform for free to users
100% Transparent: Our commission never influences which experiences we recommend. We only list experiences that meet our strict sustainability criteria.

Bookings & Payments

All bookings are handled externally through verified booking platforms and tour operators. Here's the process:

  1. Browse - Explore sustainable experiences on HygGeo
  2. Click "Book Now" - You'll be redirected to the official booking site
  3. Complete booking - Book directly with the provider on their secure platform
  4. Confirmation - You'll receive confirmation from the booking provider (not HygGeo)
  5. Enjoy - Take your sustainable adventure!

Important: HygGeo does not process payments, hold reservations, or handle cancellations. All of these are managed by the experience provider.

No, you never pay HygGeo directly.

When you book an experience through our site, you complete the transaction with:

  • The tour operator's booking system
  • A third-party booking platform (like Viator, GetYourGuide, etc.)
  • The accommodation provider's reservation system

Your payment information is processed securely by these established booking platforms - HygGeo never sees or stores your payment details.

Since all bookings are made directly with experience providers, cancellations and refunds are handled by them, not HygGeo.

To cancel or request a refund:

  1. Review the cancellation policy shown on the booking page before booking
  2. Contact the booking platform or tour operator directly
  3. Follow their specific cancellation procedures
  4. Refunds are processed according to the provider's policy
Tip: Always read the cancellation policy carefully before booking. Policies vary by provider.

Sustainability & Curation

We carefully curate every experience on HygGeo using our Sustainability Scorecard. Each experience is evaluated on:

  • Environmental Impact - Carbon footprint, waste management, resource conservation
  • Local Community Support - Fair wages, local employment, community benefits
  • Cultural Authenticity - Respect for local cultures and traditions
  • Conservation Efforts - Wildlife protection, habitat preservation
  • Certifications - Recognized eco-certifications (LEED, Green Key, etc.)

We also verify provider credentials, check reviews, and may conduct site visits for featured experiences.

Hygge (pronounced "hoo-gah") is a Danish concept that embodies coziness, contentment, and well-being through simple pleasures.

In travel, hygge means:

  • Mindful moments - Slowing down to truly experience each destination
  • Authentic connections - Building genuine relationships with locals and fellow travelers
  • Simple pleasures - Finding joy in cozy cafes, scenic walks, and meaningful conversations
  • Sustainable choices - Traveling responsibly to preserve these special moments for future generations

At HygGeo, we believe the best travel combines hygge's mindful approach with environmental responsibility.
